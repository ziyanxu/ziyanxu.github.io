And this should be my introduction

I am particularly interested in protoplanetary disks, the disks surrounding young stars consist of gas and dust, which are believed to be the birthplaces of planet. I work with Prof. Gregory Herczeg and Prof. Xue-Ning Bai. I study protoplanetary disk wind by comparing observational results with modeling. I also perform MHD simulations to study planet formation processes including streaming instability and pebble accretion.
